# Problem: Speaking Too Quickly



## What this means

Speaking very quickly can make it harder for listeners to follow your ideas, even if your content is strong.



## Why it happens

- Excitement or nervousness

- Trying to fit in too much information

- Lack of awareness of pacing



## What you can try

- Aim to pause briefly at sentence boundaries

- Emphasize key words instead of rushing through them

- Practice speaking slightly slower than feels natural



## Simple exercise

Read a short paragraph aloud while timing yourself. Repeat it again while intentionally slowing down by about 10–15%.



## Important note

This observation reflects common speaking patterns and is not a diagnosis of any speech condition.
